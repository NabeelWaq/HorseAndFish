Stuff. Everyone has it.

We accumulate it over the course of our lives and it follows us everywhere.

At some point we have to move it.


We all want to hand over responsibility to someone we trust, to get our stuff from A to B quickly and safely.

What's Our Story

AnyVan got its start in 2009, when founder Angus discovered that 50% of vans on the road were travelling half empty. He realised that filling those empty vans would create cheaper delivery costs and it would be better for the environment. Now, 15 years on, we’ve celebrated some amazing milestones!
